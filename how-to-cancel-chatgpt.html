Cancel on iOS

If subscribed through App Store:

  1. Open Settings
  2. Tap your name (Apple ID)
  3. Tap Subscriptions
  4. Find ChatGPT
  5. Tap Cancel Subscription

Cancel on Android

If subscribed through Google Play:

  1. Open Google Play Store
  2. Tap profile icon
  3. Tap Payments and subscriptions
  4. Tap Subscriptions
  5. Find ChatGPT, tap Cancel
