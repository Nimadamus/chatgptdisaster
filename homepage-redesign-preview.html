Source Labels For Serious Claims

The site covers lawsuits, deaths, medical issues, and alleged harms. Every sensitive page should clearly show what kind of evidence it is using.

Verified fact

Confirmed by a primary record or reputable source.

Court filing

Claim appears in a legal complaint, brief, or order.

Allegation

Unproven claim that needs careful attribution.

User report

First-person or community report, not independently verified.

Analysis

Editorial interpretation or synthesis.
