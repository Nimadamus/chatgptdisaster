Why ChatGPT Goes Down So Often

ChatGPT experiences frequent outages for several reasons:

  • Massive Scale: 800+ million weekly users straining infrastructure
  • Rapid Feature Additions: GPT-5, image generation, and new features introduce instability
  • Computational Demands: LLMs require enormous processing power per query
  • Peak Usage Spikes: Business hours in US/Europe cause predictable overloads
  • Cost Pressure: OpenAI reportedly loses money on many queries, limiting infrastructure investment
