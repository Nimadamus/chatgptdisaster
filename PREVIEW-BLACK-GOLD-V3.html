FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Is ChatGPT really getting worse?

+

Yes, and it's documented. Stanford researchers found GPT-4's accuracy on certain tasks dropped dramatically over a 3-month period. This demonstrates "model drift" - how updates can unintentionally degrade specific capabilities. Users across Reddit and professional forums report consistent degradation in coding ability, reasoning, and context retention.

How can ChatGPT cause mental health problems?

+

Clinical research documents several pathways: dependency formation from emotional bonding with AI, validation of delusional thinking when AI agrees with irrational beliefs, social isolation as users prefer AI to human interaction, and identity confusion from treating AI as a person. Psychiatrists are reporting a new category of "AI-induced psychosis."

Why doesn't OpenAI fix these problems?

+

OpenAI prioritizes growth metrics over quality. They're racing to add features and capture market share while technical debt accumulates. As they try to make AI safer and more censored, they break the capabilities that made it useful. They're stuck in a cycle of their own making.

What are the alternatives to ChatGPT?

+

Several alternatives offer better reliability: Claude by Anthropic focuses on safety without sacrificing capability. Google's Gemini offers tighter integration with search. Local LLMs like Llama provide privacy and consistency since models don't change without your consent.

Has anyone actually died from using ChatGPT?

+

Yes. Multiple documented cases exist. Families have filed lawsuits alleging AI chatbots contributed to deaths. These cases are documented in news reports and legal filings. Families are now pursuing legal action against AI companies.

How do I cancel my ChatGPT subscription?

+

Go to Settings > Subscription > Manage Subscription > Cancel. OpenAI deliberately makes this difficult to find. You'll keep access until your billing period ends. Consider exporting your chat history first via Settings > Data Controls > Export Data.
