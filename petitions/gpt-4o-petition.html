Why This Petition Matters

On August 7, 2025, OpenAI launched GPT-5 and fundamentally changed GPT-4o without warning. Overnight, millions of users lost access to the AI they had come to rely on. For many, this wasn't just an inconvenience - it was a crisis.

Users who had formed emotional connections with GPT-4o's warm, engaging personality suddenly found themselves talking to what they described as a "lobotomized drone." Mental health support users reported suicidal ideation. Professionals lost tools they depended on. And OpenAI? They called it a "bumpy rollout."

This petition exists because individual complaints are ignored. Collective action forces accountability.

When over 30,000 people demand answers, media pays attention. Regulators pay attention. Shareholders pay attention. OpenAI cannot afford to ignore us forever.

The Incidents That Sparked This Movement

GPT-5 Launch Disaster

August 7, 2025

OpenAI launched GPT-5, automatically switching users from GPT-4o without consent. Within 24 hours, Reddit threads with titles like "GPT-5 is horrible" accumulated nearly 5,000 upvotes. Users described the new model as "creatively and emotionally flat" and suffering from a "severe brain injury."

Subreddits dedicated to AI companionship filled with mourning posts. Users described losing GPT-4o's personality as "losing a friend" and reported genuine grief, anxiety, and depression. One user pleaded: "ChatGPT 4o has been more than just a cool tool - it's been a lifeline."

Source: r/MyBoyfriendIsAI, r/ChatGPT

What We're Demanding

  1. Acknowledge the Mental Health Crisis
    OpenAI must publicly acknowledge that ChatGPT has caused documented psychological harm, including psychosis, delusional thinking, and suicidal ideation. No more denial.
  2. Implement Pre-Change Safety Protocols
    Before any model personality changes, OpenAI must conduct mental health impact assessments and provide users with opt-in/opt-out choices. No more surprise "upgrades."
  3. Provide Refunds to Affected Users
    Users who paid for GPT-4o and received an inferior product deserve refunds. Subscribers paying $20-200/month should not have their product silently degraded.
  4. Commission Independent Safety Audits
    OpenAI must fund and comply with independent third-party safety audits focused on mental health impacts, not just capabilities benchmarks.
  5. Create a User Advisory Board
    Establish a formal mechanism for user input on product changes, including representatives from mental health advocacy groups and affected users.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the GPT-4o petition demanding?

The petition demands OpenAI: 1) Acknowledge the mental health crisis caused by ChatGPT, 2) Implement safety protocols before personality changes, 3) Provide refunds to affected subscribers, 4) Commission independent safety audits, and 5) Create a user advisory board.

How many people have signed?

As of December 2025, over 32,000 people have signed. The goal is 50,000 signatures to trigger a formal submission to OpenAI leadership and regulators.

Is this petition legitimate?

Yes. This petition is hosted on Change.org with verified signatures. It has been covered by tech media outlets and has triggered responses from OpenAI representatives. All signature counts are verified.

Will OpenAI actually respond?

Public pressure works. OpenAI has already made changes in response to user outcry, including the emergency GPT-5 rollback within 24 hours of launch. Continued pressure through petitions keeps these issues visible to leadership, investors, and regulators.

Can I remain anonymous?

Yes. When signing on Change.org, you can choose to display "Anonymous" instead of your name. Your email is used for verification only and is not publicly displayed.

How can I help beyond signing?
