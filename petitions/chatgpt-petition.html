Documented Issues

Silent Model Degradation

Users report responses getting shorter, less detailed, and more generic over time. The Stanford study confirmed GPT-4's accuracy dropped from 97.6% to 2.4% on the same tasks in just 3 months.

Features Removed Without Notice

Plugins discontinued. Model selection removed. Advanced features quietly taken away while prices stay the same or increase.

Aggressive Rate Limiting

Pro subscribers paying $200/month report hitting rate limits within the first hour of use. "Unlimited" access that isn't unlimited.

No Refund Policy

When users complain about degraded service, OpenAI offers no refunds. You paid for a product, received something worse, and have no recourse.

What We're Demanding

  1. Refunds for Degraded Service
    Subscribers who experienced documented performance decline deserve partial or full refunds.
  2. Transparent Model Versioning
    Clear disclosure when models change. No silent swaps. Users must know what they're getting.
  3. Opt-Out Rights
    Users must be able to opt out of "safety" modifications that degrade functionality.
  4. Accurate Advertising
    OpenAI must stop advertising capabilities their products no longer have.
  5. Independent Performance Audits
    Third-party verification of product capabilities, not just OpenAI's marketing claims.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I get a refund from OpenAI?

Currently, OpenAI's refund policy is extremely restrictive. This petition aims to pressure them into offering refunds for documented service degradation. In the meantime, you may have success disputing charges with your credit card company.

Is this petition just about money?

It's about accountability. When a company takes your money and delivers an inferior product, that's fraud. The petition demands both financial restitution and systemic changes to prevent future degradation.

What proof is there that ChatGPT is getting worse?

The Stanford/UC Berkeley study documented a 95% accuracy drop in GPT-4 over 3 months. Thousands of user testimonies corroborate this. Reddit threads with thousands of upvotes document the decline in real-time.
