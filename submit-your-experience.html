Why We Collect These Accounts

This project documents AI chatbot incidents to help researchers, journalists, policymakers, and the public understand the current limitations and failure modes of AI systems.

Your experience, whether it involves hallucinated information, unexpected behavior, service failures, or other issues, contributes to a growing body of evidence that helps inform better AI development and usage practices.

What We Are Looking For

  • Factual hallucinations: AI provided false information presented as fact
  • Legal or professional issues: AI-generated content caused real-world problems
  • Service reliability issues: Outages, errors, or unexpected behavior
  • Safety concerns: Inappropriate, harmful, or concerning AI responses
  • Enterprise or business impacts: AI failures affecting organizations
  • Any documented incident with verifiable details
